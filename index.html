Welcome

Sanrio is a Japanese company founded by Shintaro Tsuji, focusing on the kawaii segment of Japanese popular culture. This website is dedicated to the Sanrio characters, specifically Hello Kitty, and her friends: My Melody, Kuromi, Cinnamoroll and Pompompurin. I will present their profiles on the Characters page. On the Form page you can submit a form with your own favorite Sanrio character! Check out the About me page for more information about the creator of this site. Have fun!
